# OGame Strategic Knowledge Base (RAG)

## 🎯 Core Game Strategies

### 1. **Fleetsaving - The Foundation**
- **Critical Rule**: Never leave fleet visible when offline
- **Methods**:
  - **Harvest**: Send fleet to debris fields with recall
  - **Deploy**: Most secure - not visible in phalanx even when returning
  - **Colonization**: Long-distance missions to position 16
  - **Expedition**: Position 16 in any system, 1-hour duration optimal
  - **Attack**: Slow ships against inactive targets for timing

### 2. **Playing Styles**

#### **Miner Strategy**
- **Focus**: Resource production optimization
- **Advantages**: Less time-intensive, steady progression, predictable growth
- **Key Priorities**:
  - Upgrade mines in optimal order (typically Metal > Crystal > Deuterium)
  - Position colonies in slots 4-8 for better resource production
  - Research: Energy Tech → Plasma Tech → mine efficiency techs
  - Fleet mainly for resource transport and fleetsaving
- **Transition**: Eventually becomes Turtle or Fleeter when mine ROI diminishes

#### **Fleeter Strategy**  
- **Focus**: Fleet building and raiding
- **Advantages**: Fast resource acquisition, combat control, high excitement
- **Key Priorities**:
  - Combat technologies (Weapons, Shields, Armor)
  - Rapid Research for ship unlocks
  - Strategic positioning for raid opportunities
  - Master fleetsaving techniques
- **Risk**: High - total loss possible, requires constant attention

#### **Turtle Strategy**
- **Focus**: Defense and resource protection
- **Advantages**: Very safe, good for casual play
- **Key Elements**:
  - Heavy defense investment
  - Strong mine foundation
  - Diplomatic protection (alliances)
- **Limitation**: Defense has no ROI, purely protective

### 3. **Resource Management**

#### **Production Optimization**
- **Mine Ratios**: Aim for 2:1:1 (Metal:Crystal:Deuterium) production
- **Energy Balance**: Solar plants early game, fusion reactors late game
- **Colony Placement**: Slots 1-3 (hot) for deuterium, 4-6 (optimal) for balanced, 7-9 (cold) for energy
- **Transport Efficiency**: Position colonies for synchronized resource collection

#### **Spending Priorities**
1. **Early Game**: Mines to level 10-15, basic research
2. **Mid Game**: Advanced research, fleet/defense based on strategy
3. **Late Game**: High-level specialized buildings, moon development

### 4. **Combat & Fleet Composition**

#### **Basic Combat Mechanics**
- **Battle Rounds**: Maximum 6 rounds
- **Targeting**: Random within ship classes
- **Debris**: 30% of destroyed ships become debris
- **Fuel Consumption**: Deuterium cost for all movements

#### **Optimal Fleet Compositions**

**Raiding Fleet (Anti-Defense)**:
- Light Fighters + Cruisers (early game)
- Battleships + Battlecruisers (mid game)
- Destroyers + Death Stars (late game heavy defenses)

**Anti-Fleet Compositions**:
- Destroyers vs Heavy Fighters/Cruisers  
- Bombers vs Battleships
- Death Stars vs everything (overkill but guaranteed)

**Resource Raids**:
- Large Cargo Ships + escort (Light Fighters minimum)
- Calculate cargo capacity vs target resources
- Always simulate battles before attacking

### 5. **Expedition Strategy**

#### **Optimal Expedition Setup**
- **Fleet Size**: ~200-300 ships for best resource/ship finds
- **Composition**: 
  - 256 Large Cargo Ships + 1 Destroyer (popular setup)
  - Mix of combat ships for alien encounters
- **Duration**: Always 1 hour (longer = waste of time)
- **Location Rotation**: Switch systems every 2-3 days to avoid black holes

#### **Expected Returns**
- **Profitability**: Highly profitable with daily expeditions
- **Risk Management**: ~1% ship loss rate, profits far exceed losses
- **Dark Matter**: Valuable secondary reward, especially during events

### 6. **Research Priorities**

#### **Universal Research Path**:
1. **Energy Technology** (level 3) - Unlocks key techs
2. **Combustion Drive** (level 6) - Basic ship movement
3. **Espionage Technology** (level 2-4) - Intelligence gathering
4. **Computer Technology** (level 2+) - Fleet slots
5. **Weapons/Shields/Armor** - Combat effectiveness
6. **Hyperspace Technology** - Advanced ships
7. **Plasma Technology** - End-game efficiency

#### **Specialization Paths**:
- **Miners**: Focus on Plasma Tech, Energy Tech, mine efficiency
- **Fleeters**: Prioritize combat techs, ship unlocks
- **Explorers**: Expedition Tech, Astrophysics for colonies

### 7. **Advanced Strategies**

#### **Moon Development**
- **Creation**: Minimum 20% chance with 1.5M+ debris
- **Uses**: Phalanx sensors, Jump Gates, secure fleetsave
- **Priority Buildings**: Sensor Phalanx, Moon Hangars, Jump Gates

#### **Alliance Warfare**
- **ACS (Alliance Combat System)**: Coordinated attacks/defenses
- **Strategy**: Combined fleetsaves, mutual protection
- **Diplomacy**: NAPs, trade agreements, coordinated expansion

#### **Economic Warfare**
- **Market Manipulation**: Resource trading for advantage
- **Blockades**: Preventing enemy resource collection
- **Infrastructure Targeting**: Attacking mines vs fleets

### 8. **Automation & Tools**

#### **Combat Simulation**
- **Tools**: OSimulate, OGame Tools combat simulator
- **Usage**: Always simulate before attacks
- **Optimization**: Test different fleet compositions

#### **Profit Calculations**
- **ROI Analysis**: Mine upgrade payback periods  
- **Raid Efficiency**: Deuterium cost vs resource gain
- **Time Value**: Resource/hour calculations

#### **Timing Optimization**
- **Flight Times**: Coordinate multiple operations
- **Production Cycles**: Align building/research with activity
- **Event Planning**: Maximize during special events

## 🚨 **Critical Success Factors**

### **Risk Management**
1. **Never leave fleet visible when offline**
2. **Always fleetsave with resources**  
3. **Simulate all combat before attacking**
4. **Diversify colonies to spread risk**
5. **Maintain diplomatic relations**

### **Efficiency Principles**
1. **Optimize for your available time**
2. **Focus on ROI for all investments**
3. **Automate routine calculations**
4. **Plan 2-3 moves ahead**
5. **Adapt strategy based on universe dynamics**

### **Growth Patterns**
1. **Exponential Growth Phase**: Early mine development
2. **Specialization Phase**: Choose miner/fleeter/turtle
3. **Optimization Phase**: Efficiency improvements  
4. **Dominance Phase**: Large-scale operations
5. **Endgame**: Alliance warfare and universe control
